Who we are

Heal & Nourish started the way most good things do — in a kitchen, with a person who cared. We are a brother-and-sister team from South Africa with a simple belief: good food should be available to everyone, not just people who can afford a Woolworths run.

We started with seamoss — a nutrient powerhouse that has been used for generations. Now we are building toward something bigger: a range of ancient grain flours and wholefood ingredients that bring real nutrition back to everyday cooking.

Everything we make is organic, plant-based, and made with as much care as we can manage in a small batch. That will not change.
